About Echofy

Echofy is a browser extension designed to help users evaluate the credibility and bias of online content. It analyzes selected text for potential misinformation indicators, bias patterns, and AI-generated content markers.

How It Works

  • Select text on any webpage
  • Right-click and choose "Analyze with Echofy"
  • View credibility scores, bias analysis, and fact-check resources
  • Access your analysis history for reference

Privacy & Data

Echofy processes text locally in your browser. No text content is sent to external servers. Analysis history is stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time.
